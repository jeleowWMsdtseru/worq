Syllabus Overview

Day 1: Foundations & No-Code Automation

  • Fundamentals of AI Agents: Dive into core concepts like Large Language Models (LLMs), memory, and tools.
  • Automation Strategy: Understand risk-benefit tradeoffs and identify high-impact opportunities in your business.
  • Effective Prompting Techniques: Learn how to craft prompts that get you the precise results you need from AI.
  • Hands-on with n8n: Build your first automated workflow and a functional chatbot from the ground up.

Day 2: Deployment & Scaling

  • Security: Understand how to run locally if needed for enhanced privacy and control.
  • Building Smart Internal Tools: Use Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to create intelligent internal bots.
  • Real-World Deployment: Implement six different production-ready automations for common business tasks.
  • Group Exercise: Design and build your own DIY workflows using our expert-provided templates.
